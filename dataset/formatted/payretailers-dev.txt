https://payretailers.dev/ PayRetailers is a payment processing company that offers payment solutions to businesses for LATAM and African countries. Through us you will be able to use various payment methods to process transactions and payouts. Transactions , also called deposits, refer to payments made from your customers to your shop. Payouts , also called withdrawals, refer to payments made from your shop to your customer's account. We offer REST API - based. In both cases, you will need API keys and Subscription Key to start processing.
